# Instagram Music Selection Guide for Carousel Posts

## Why Music Matters for Carousels

- Posts with music receive ~15-30% more reach than posts without
- Instagram's algorithm interprets music as "higher effort content" and rewards it
- Music creates "audio branding" -- viewers associate your sound with your content
- Saved carousel posts inherit the audio, extending reach on revisit

## Trending Audio Strategy

### When to Use Trending Audio

1. **Discovery Phase** (Day 0-3): <1K posts using it. Maximum algorithmic boost. JUMP ON IT if it fits.
2. **Growth Phase** (Day 3-14): 1K-50K posts. Still strong boost. Best window for most creators.
3. **Peak Phase** (Day 14-30): 50K-500K posts. Reduced boost, strong recognition.
4. **Saturation Phase** (Day 30+): >500K posts. No boost. Skip unless it genuinely matches.

### The Rule

**Only use trending audio if it genuinely fits your content type and audience.** A mismatched trending sound feels desperate and damages trust. Authenticity > algorithm.

### How to Find Trending Audio

1. Open Instagram Reels tab
2. Look for the trending arrow icon next to audio names
3. Check the Audio page -- number of Reels using it indicates lifecycle stage
4. In the music picker when creating a post, "Trending" section appears first

## Instagram Music Picker Tips

When adding music to a carousel post on Instagram:
1. Tap "Add music" during post creation
2. Search for the recommended track name + artist
3. If exact track is unavailable, search the GENRE KEYWORDS listed above
4. Choose a 15-30 second clip that matches the emotional peak of your carousel
5. For educational/text-heavy carousels, pick the most ambient/instrumental section
6. Instagram shows "Popular" suggestions -- use these ONLY if they match your content type
